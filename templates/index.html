Welcome to SoWise!

SoWise is a Prediction Market built by HKUST students and can analyze the ability and cost of a given set of social media users when they are treated as potential "workers" for crowdsourcing tasks. Moreover, the SoWise has two built-in mechanisms to conduct the enrollment of such "workers", namely "pay-as-you- go" and "prediction market". For each mechanism, an optimal set of users can be selected according to specific requirement of quality or budget.
